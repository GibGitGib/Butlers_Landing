Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 26, 2026  ·  Last updated: June 26, 2026

Business Butlers ("we," "us," or "our") is a service operated by CoreTech Studios. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you interact with our website and consulting services.

1. What We Collect

When you submit our intake form to request consulting services, we collect the information you voluntarily provide:

  • Name — so we know who we're talking to
  • Email address — so we can reply to you
  • Company name — to understand your business context
  • Role / title — to know your position in the organization
  • Industry — to tailor our recommendations
  • Pain points — so we understand what needs fixing
  • Services checked — which AI consulting services interest you
We do not collect anything you don't give us. There is no tracking, no analytics, no profiling, no advertising pixels, and no third-party data sources feeding into our systems. No backend server stores or processes your data automatically — submissions are handled through a form service (see Section 3).

2. How We Use Your Data

We use the information you provide strictly for the following purposes:

  • To respond to your inquiry and provide the consulting services you requested
  • To prepare and send custom proposals or recommendations based on your stated needs
  • To follow up with you about our services (only when you've expressed interest)
  • To communicate about ongoing engagements if you become a client

We do not use your data for marketing automation, newsletters (unless you separately opt in), or to build profiles for resale. We do not share your intake data with any third party except as necessary to deliver the services described below.

3. Third-Party Services

We use a small set of third-party tools to operate. Your data only passes through these services when necessary for the function you're engaging with:

Formspree — Our intake form submissions are processed by Formspree for email delivery. When you fill out our form, your data is transmitted to Formspree's servers to route it to our inbox. See Formspree's Privacy Policy.

Stripe — If you become a paying client, payment processing is handled by Stripe. Stripe receives transaction data necessary to process payments but does not receive your intake form details. See Stripe's Privacy Policy.

Gumroad — If we distribute license keys or digital products through Gumroad, Gumroad processes the purchase transaction. See Gumroad's Privacy Policy.

No data is shared with these services unless you engage with them. Browsing our site does not trigger calls to Stripe, Gumroad, or analytics scripts. Payments and form submissions only involve third parties at the point of transaction.

4. Cookies

We use minimal, functional cookies to make the site work:

  • Form state cookie — preserves your progress on our intake form so you don't lose information if you navigate away. Contains no personal data — just which form step you're on. Session-only.
  • Consent cookie — remembers that you've acknowledged our cookie notice. Expires after 12 months.

We do not use tracking cookies, analytics cookies, advertising cookies, or any third-party cookies. For full details, see our Cookie Policy.

5. Data Retention

We retain intake form submissions for as long as necessary to fulfill the consulting engagement or follow-up conversation they relate to. Typical retention periods:

  • Active clients — data retained for the duration of the engagement plus 12 months after completion, for reference and continuity.
  • Inquiries that don't convert — data retained for up to 6 months after last contact, then deleted.
  • Cookie data — session cookies are deleted when you close your browser. Consent cookies expire after 12 months.

Because we do not operate a backend database, your intake data lives in email records (Formspree submissions delivered to our inbox). Deletion means removing those emails from our systems.

6. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have rights including:

  • Access — request a copy of the personal data we hold about you
  • Correction — ask us to fix inaccurate or incomplete data
  • Deletion — request that we delete your personal data
  • Objection — object to certain processing of your data
  • Portability — receive your data in a structured, machine-readable format

To exercise any of these rights, or if you have questions about your data, email us at the address below. We will respond within 30 days.

7. Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your information. Our site is served over HTTPS. Form submissions are transmitted over encrypted connections to Formspree. Payment processing is handled by Stripe, which maintains industry-standard security certifications (PCI-DSS Level 1).

However, no method of electronic transmission or storage is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security of your data.

8. Children's Privacy

Our services are directed at business owners and professionals. We do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone under 16 years of age. If you believe a minor has provided us with personal data, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will revise the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Material changes will be noted conspicuously. Continued use of our services after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
